Export Markets

Exporting to Six Regions

Certified for each market. Documented for each destination. Teknic products ship with full compliance documentation — datasheets, certification copies, and test reports — tailored to international buyer requirements.

Export Region 01
Middle East
Supplying OEMs and distributors across UAE, Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Qatar, Bahrain, and Oman. Strong demand for UL and CSA certified circuit breakers and micro switches in panel building and industrial applications.
Export Region 02
Africa
Export capability to South Africa, Nigeria, Kenya, Egypt, and East Africa. ISO 9001 and Intertek certifications are key requirements for African market entry and are held by Teknic across its full product range.
Export Region 03
Europe
TÜV and VDE approved rocker switches for European OEMs. EN 60934 certified circuit breakers for European panel and equipment builders. Full CE-relevant documentation available on request.
Export Region 04
United Kingdom
UKAS accredited ISO 9001 certification gives UK buyers the highest confidence in our quality system. TÜV and VDE approved products meet UK market requirements for electrical component safety.
Export Region 05
USA & Canada
UL-1077 listed circuit breakers and UL/CSA certified micro switches for North American OEMs. Full UL and CSA certification documentation provided. Compliance with NEC and CEC requirements.
Export Region 06
Southeast Asia
Supplying to Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, Indonesia, Vietnam and the Philippines. Intertek certification widely recognised across ASEAN markets. Strong OEM demand for UL and TÜV certified components.
