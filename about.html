Our Story

A Pioneer in Indian
Electromechanical Manufacturing

Teknic Electromeconics Pvt Ltd was established in 1971 — at a time when India's electronics industry was in its infancy. What began as a precision plastics processing unit supplying Philips India evolved, through rigorous investment in tooling, testing, and certification, into one of India's foremost manufacturers of low-voltage switchgear components.

Today, operating from our factory at Electronic City Phase 1, Bengaluru, we manufacture circuit breakers, micro switches, rocker switches, and foot switches that meet the most demanding international standards — UL, CSA, TÜV, VDE, and ISO 9001.

Company History

Over Five Decades of Manufacturing

From a precision plastics unit supplying Philips India to a globally certified switchgear manufacturer — built on engineering discipline since 1971.

1971
Founded in Bengaluru
Teknic Electromeconics Pvt Ltd established as a precision plastic processing unit in Bengaluru, manufacturing components for India's growing electronics industry.
Early years
Supplier to Philips India
Teknic supplied precision plastic components to Philips India, building quality discipline and manufacturing rigour that would underpin every product that followed.
1989
Entering Electromechanical Products
Drawing on deep expertise in precision plastic moulding and tooling, Teknic entered the electromechanical components sector — developing the micro switches and circuit breakers that form our core range today.
Electronic City, Bengaluru
Full In-House Manufacturing
Factory at 93, Electronic City Phase 1 — 5th Cross, Hosur Road — houses the complete manufacturing chain: tool room, mould design, injection moulding, metal stamping, assembly, and final testing, all under one roof.
Certifications

Teknic holds seven internationally recognised certifications — ISO 9001 (UKAS accredited), UL, CSA, TÜV, VDE, and Intertek — covering North America, Europe, and global markets.
Today
PAN India Supply. Global Export.
Teknic Electromeconics supplies OEMs across India and exports to the Middle East, Africa, Europe, UK, USA & Canada, and Southeast Asia. 50+ models, 7 certifications, and over 50 years of manufacturing behind every product.
Manufacturing

In-House from Concept
to Finished Product

Our factory at Electronic City Phase 1 runs every stage of production internally — giving us full control over quality, lead times, and customisation.

Tool Room
In-house tool room with precision machining capability. All moulds and stamping tools designed and maintained internally for tight tolerance and rapid iteration.
Mould Design
Custom mould design capability for plastic housing components. Engineered for dimensional accuracy, material compatibility, and long production life.
Injection Moulding
Precision thermoplastic injection moulding for switch bodies, housings, and actuator components. Process-controlled for consistency across high production volumes.
Metal Stamping
Sheet metal stamping for contact elements, terminal blades, and structural components. High-speed progressive dies ensure repeatability and close tolerances.
Assembly
Controlled assembly of electromechanical sub-assemblies and finished products. Trained operators follow certified procedures for every product family.
In-House Testing
Final inspection and testing lab on-site. Electrical, mechanical, and environmental tests performed to UL, CSA, TÜV, and VDE test standards before despatch.
Certifications

Certified for Every
Major Market

Seven internationally recognised certifications covering North America, Europe, UK, and global markets. Every certification is maintained through regular audits and product testing.

ISO 9001
Quality Management System
Our quality management system is certified to ISO 9001 — the internationally recognised benchmark for manufacturing quality systems. Accredited by UKAS, ensuring our certification meets the highest standards of impartiality and rigour.
UKAS
UK Accreditation Service
UKAS accreditation of our ISO 9001 certification provides international buyers with the highest level of confidence. UKAS is the sole national accreditation body recognised by the UK government.
UL
Underwriters Laboratories (USA)
UL listing is mandatory for sale in the USA and widely recognised globally. Our circuit breakers (UL-1077) and micro switches are UL listed — providing electrical safety assurance for North American OEMs.
CSA
Canadian Standards Association
CSA certification (CSA 22.2) is required for equipment sold in Canada. Our circuit breakers and micro switches carry CSA approval, making them fully compliant for Canadian market specifications.
TÜV
TÜV Rheinland / SÜD
TÜV approval is the European safety benchmark for electrical components. Our rocker switches carry TÜV approval, enabling sale and specification across the EU and in markets that require European safety marks.
VDE
Verband der Elektrotechnik (Germany)
VDE certification is one of the most stringent European electrical component marks. German and EU OEMs requiring VDE-tested components can specify Teknic with confidence.
Intertek
Total Quality Assurance
Intertek certification provides a further layer of global quality assurance, recognised by buyers across Southeast Asia, the Middle East, and Africa who require third-party verified product compliance.
