title: "About Teknic Electromeconics Pvt Ltd"
body: |
  Teknic Electromeconics Pvt Ltd was established in 1971 in Bengaluru, Karnataka, India. What began as a precision plastics processing unit supplying Philips India evolved into one of India's foremost manufacturers of low-voltage switchgear components.

  In 1989, Teknic entered the electromechanical products sector — developing micro switches and circuit breakers that now carry UL, CSA, TÜV, VDE, and ISO 9001 certifications.

  Our factory at 93, Electronic City Phase 1, Bengaluru houses the complete manufacturing chain: tool room, mould design, injection moulding, metal stamping, assembly, and final testing — all under one roof.

  Teknic holds seven internationally recognised certifications — ISO 9001 (UKAS accredited), UL, CSA, TÜV, VDE, and Intertek — and supplies OEMs across India and internationally.
